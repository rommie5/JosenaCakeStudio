Our Overview

Josena Cake Studio is a vibrant, community-oriented entreprise specialising in cake production, sales, and professional training. It serves as a dual-purpose facility.

  • 1.Cake production and Sales: High quality cakes for various occasions, including weddings, birthdays and corporate events.
  • Prioritizing user satisfaction with seamless, intuitive delliveries.
  • Training : Practical and theoretical training in cake artistry, aimed at empowering individuals with employable skills and entreprenuerial capabilities.
